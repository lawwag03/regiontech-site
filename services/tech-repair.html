Common Problems

Sound familiar? We fix that.

You don't need to know the technical term. Pick the description that matches what's happening.

🐌

"My computer is really slow"

Takes forever to start up, freezes when you open anything, or just feels like it's running through mud. Usually fixable without buying a new machine.

🚫

"It won't turn on"

Press the power button and nothing happens — or it starts to turn on and then shuts right back off. We diagnose what's causing it before doing any repairs.

🔄

"It keeps restarting on its own"

Randomly shuts down or restarts without warning. Usually a sign of overheating, a failing part, or a software problem — all things we can check and fix.

🦠

"I think I have a virus"

Seeing pop-ups, weird messages, or your browser going to strange websites? We scan for and remove malware, then show you how to avoid it going forward.

📶

"It won't connect to Wi-Fi"

Everyone else's device works fine but yours won't connect — or it connects but drops constantly. We troubleshoot the device, not just the router.

🖨️

"My printer won't work"

Says it's offline, won't find the printer, or just ignores you when you hit print. Printers are notoriously frustrating — we sort them out.

💾

"It says the disk is full"

Running out of storage space means your device slows to a crawl. We clear out what's safe to remove and can upgrade your storage if needed.

🆕

"I got a new device and need help"

Got a new computer, tablet, or phone and need it set up properly? We handle the full setup — apps, accounts, passwords, and moving your files over from the old one.

Devices

What we work on

Not sure if we handle your specific device? Just ask — if it's something we can help with, we'll tell you. If it's outside what we do, we'll point you somewhere that can.

Windows Laptops & Desktops

Any brand — Dell, HP, Lenovo, Acer, ASUS, and more. Slow, broken, or just needs a tune-up.

  • Won't turn on or keeps crashing
  • Running slow or freezing up
  • Viruses, pop-ups, or weird behavior
  • Screen, keyboard, or hardware damage
  • Storage upgrade (make it feel new again)

Mac Computers

MacBooks and iMacs — Apple devices running slow, not starting up, or needing a fresh setup.

  • Running very slow (common on older Macs)
  • Won't turn on or freezes at startup
  • Storage full and can't clear it
  • New Mac setup and data transfer

Chromebooks

The simple laptops that run Google — popular in schools and for everyday browsing.

  • Won't connect to Wi-Fi or school network
  • Forgot Google account password
  • Running slow or freezing
  • Screen or keyboard issues
  • Setup for a student or new user

iPads & Android Tablets

Tablets used for browsing, streaming, video calls, and everything in between.

  • Frozen or not responding to touch
  • Forgot passcode or Apple ID
  • Apps crashing or not loading
  • Setup for a parent or grandparent
  • Transfer photos and files to a new tablet

Printers

The device that somehow causes more frustration than anything else in the house.

  • Says "offline" even though it's on
  • Computer can't find the printer
  • Wireless printing not working
  • Paper jams or feeding problems
  • Setup on a new computer or network

Gaming Systems

PlayStation, Xbox, Nintendo Switch — when your console isn't cooperating.

  • Won't connect to the internet or Wi-Fi
  • Overheating or shutting off mid-game
  • Disc reader not working
  • Controller connectivity issues
  • Setup on a new TV or network

Smartphones

iPhones and Android phones — setup, transfers, and troubleshooting (we don't do screen replacements).

  • Setting up a new phone
  • Moving contacts, photos, and apps from old phone
  • Won't connect to Wi-Fi or Bluetooth
  • Apps not working or crashing
  • Account recovery help

Smart Home Devices

Smart TVs, streaming sticks, smart speakers, and home automation gadgets.

  • Smart TV won't connect to Wi-Fi
  • Streaming stick setup (Roku, Fire Stick, etc.)
  • Smart speaker not responding
  • Smart home devices not working together
  • New TV setup and app installation
