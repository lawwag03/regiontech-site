Overview

Old tech shouldn't end up in a landfill

Throwing electronics in the trash is illegal in many states — and your data goes with them. Region Tech provides secure local pickup of old devices across Northwest Indiana, handles data destruction properly, and ensures everything is recycled responsibly. No shipping. No drop-off hassle. We come to you.

What We Accept

Computers & Laptops

Desktops, laptops, all-in-ones — any age, any condition. Data wiped or drive destroyed before disposal.

Hard Drives & SSDs

Secure data wiping (DoD standard) or physical destruction — certified and documented on request.

Networking Gear

Routers, switches, modems, access points, and network infrastructure — reset and recycled.

Phones & Tablets

Factory reset and responsibly recycled. Includes data wipe verification before disposal.

Printers & Peripherals

Printers, scanners, monitors, keyboards, mice, and accessories — all accepted for recycling.

Misc Electronics & Cables

Old chargers, cables, power strips, gaming hardware, and other electronics no longer in use.
