Overview

Your memories are degrading right now

VHS tapes lose quality every year they sit on a shelf — and the players to watch them are disappearing fast. Region Tech converts your legacy media into modern digital files so those moments aren't lost. All done locally, handled carefully, returned to you.

What We Convert

VHS & VHS-C Tapes

Home videos, recorded movies, family moments — converted to MP4 or your preferred format.

Camcorder Tapes

Hi8, MiniDV, Digital8, and other camcorder formats digitized and preserved.

Audio Cassettes

Mixtapes, recordings, and personal audio transferred to digital MP3 or FLAC files.

Photos & Slides

High-resolution scanning of printed photos, 35mm slides, and negatives.

CD & DVD Ripping

Extract content from aging CDs and DVDs before they degrade — music, video, data.

File Format Conversion

Already digital but in the wrong format? We convert between video, audio, and image formats.
