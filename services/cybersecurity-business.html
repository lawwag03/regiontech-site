Overview

Small businesses are the most targeted

Over 40% of cyberattacks target small businesses — and most lack the IT staff to respond. Region Tech provides practical, affordable security for local businesses: network hardening, employee system protection, and clear guidance on staying safe without disrupting your workflow.

What's Included

Network Security Assessment

Audit your business network for vulnerabilities, open ports, and misconfigurations.

Employee System Hardening

Secure workstations, enforce update policies, and configure user account controls.

Access Controls & Permissions

Ensure employees only access what they need — reducing your attack surface.

Firewall & Router Configuration

Properly configure business routers and firewalls to block unauthorized access.

Email Security & Phishing Defense

Configure spam filters, set up DMARC/SPF records, and train staff on phishing tactics.

Backup & Recovery Planning

Set up reliable backups so you can recover quickly from ransomware or hardware failure.
