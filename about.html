Who We Are

About Region Tech

A local technology company built for Northwest Indiana — focused on security, sustainability, and making tech accessible to real people.

Mission

Tech that works for people, not against them

Region Tech exists because most people in Northwest Indiana don't have access to honest, affordable, local tech help. Big box stores upsell. National services ship your devices across the country. Online support is impersonal and slow.

We built Region Tech to be different — a local company that shows up, does the work, charges a fair flat rate, and treats your data and devices with the same care we'd want for our own.

What We Stand For

Our values

Security first

Every service we provide — repair, disposal, network work — is handled with data security as a baseline, not an add-on.

Genuinely local

We live here. We work here. Your devices don't get shipped to a warehouse across the country — everything stays in Northwest Indiana.

Transparent pricing

Flat rates. Quoted upfront. No hidden fees, no surprise invoices, no upselling. You know what you're paying before we start.

Responsible disposal

We take e-waste seriously. Old electronics are handled with proper data destruction and recycled responsibly — nothing goes to landfill.

Plain English

No jargon, no condescension. We explain what's wrong, what we did, and what to watch for — in language that actually makes sense.

Fast turnaround

We respect your time. Most services are completed within 48 hours — often the same day for straightforward repairs.

The Story

Why Region Tech exists

Northwest Indiana has always been a place where people work hard, look out for each other, and expect straight answers. But when it comes to technology — getting a device fixed, understanding a security threat, or getting rid of old equipment safely — most people either overpay at a chain store or get stuck waiting weeks for a mail-in service.

Region Tech was founded to close that gap. We started with a simple idea: be the tech company that actually shows up. Local pickup. Honest pricing. Work done right.

We're not trying to be a national brand. We're building something that belongs here — a tech company that serves this specific community, understands its needs, and grows with it over time.
